## Sandbox

Freeing up space

    root@platform:~# mv /var/cache /home
    root@platform:~# cd /var
    root@platform:~# ln -sf /home/cache cache
    root@platform:~# mv /usr/share /home
    root@platform:~# cd /usr
    root@platform:~# ln -sf /home/share share
